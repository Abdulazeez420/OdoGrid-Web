BACKUP & RESTORE

Your Backup. On Your Terms.

No proprietary cloud. Back up your encrypted database directly to Google Drive or Dropbox — providers you already use.

Google Drive

"Backs up to your private appDataFolder — invisible to other apps"

  • ZIP compression & auto-deduplication
  • Timestamp tracking
  • Dedicated backup and restore screen
  • Configurable auto-backup — Daily, Weekly, or Monthly

Dropbox

"Backs up as encrypted ZIP into your /Apps/OdoGrid"

  • ZIP compression with timestamp history
  • Merge restore — only adds missing records
  • Configurable auto-backup — Daily, Weekly, or Monthly
  • Silently skips if not yet connected — never opens OAuth prompts in the background

Open the App Drawer and choose Google Drive or Dropbox to manage backup and restore from dedicated Sync pages.

Auto-backup is enabled by default after your first successful backup — no extra configuration needed. Adjust the frequency (Daily, Weekly, or Monthly) from the Backup & Sync screen at any time.

Google Drive restore replaces local data; Dropbox restore merges missing records into local data. All backup files remain encrypted end-to-end.

ABOUT ODOGRID

Built for Drivers Who Care About Their Cars

OdoGrid was built from a simple frustration — there was no single app that tracked fuel costs, upcoming maintenance, business mileage, and protected your data without demanding an account or an internet connection.

We built OdoGrid to be the app we wanted to use ourselves: offline-first, encrypted by default, with smart features that save time and money at every service station and year-end tax filing.

The app supports petrol, diesel, hybrid, and electric vehicles. It works for individual drivers, freelancers tracking business mileage, and fleet managers who need detailed cost records for multiple vehicles.
